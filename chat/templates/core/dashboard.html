Welcome to Hospital Chat!

In the year of 2016, I was hospitalized for the treatment of exercise induced compartment syndrome and rhabdomyolysis. With the help of friends and family pushing me through deliriums and 4 surgeries, I managed to pull through and make a full recovery.

As eager as I was to learn and move on from the experience, I couldn't help but think about my patient peers around the world who may not have had adequate support throughout their bare-bottom journey. During a time when you may not be able to move, eat, or speak, this support can become the differentiator between an uncomfortable experience and an utterly debilitating one.

With that in mind, I created this application to facilitate supporting patients around the world. I hope to be able to give people the power to talk with others about their illnesses or simply be voice of support to those in need.

This application is in very early development. If you have any suggestions, please email me at josuevalverde12@gmail.com


Enough story, let's chat!
